About

I'm Brandon, a product designer with a background in marketing and visual design. My interest in UX took shape while I was studying for my Advertising and Marketing diploma, and it has grown into a career focused on thoughtful, user-centred digital products.

I'm currently a Senior Product Designer at Flipp, based in Dutton, Ontario. I work closely with cross-functional teams to research problems, iterate on ideas, and ship high-fidelity solutions that address real user needs. I'm equally comfortable thinking visually and technically — from prototyping and design systems to collaborating with developers on implementation details.

Whether I'm mentoring designers, leading projects end to end, or partnering with stakeholders on clear communication, I care about building engaging experiences that are grounded in research and craft.
